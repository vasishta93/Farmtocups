Our Story

The Birth of a Farm-to-Cup Revolution

The seed of FarmtoCups was planted with a simple yet powerful idea: what if urban consumers could taste the true, unadulterated flavors of Coorg's produce? This question, born from a deep appreciation for the region's agricultural heritage, has grown into an innovative business model poised to disrupt the traditional supply chain.

At the heart of FarmtoCups is the commitment to a direct farm-to-cup approach. As we prepare for our launch, we're forging partnerships with local farmers, starting with the iconic Robusta coffee that Coorg is famous for. Ravi Kumar, a fourth-generation coffee farmer, is one of our first partners. His eyes light up as he speaks about the potential of this new venture: "For years, our coffee has passed through so many hands before reaching the consumer. With FarmtoCups, we have a chance to share our story and our passion directly with those who enjoy our coffee."

But our vision extends beyond coffee. Coorg's fertile lands yield a bounty of flavors, and FarmtoCups aims to capture them all. Our initial product line will include:
1. Artisanal Coffee: Bold, full-bodied Robusta with notes of dark chocolate
2. Exotic Teas: Handpicked and carefully processed local tea varieties
3. Fruit Juices: Made from Coorg's succulent seasonal fruits
4. Herbal Infusions: Crafted from indigenous herbs and spices
5. Specialty Drinks: Innovative blends that showcase Coorg's unique flavors

Each of these products represents not just a beverage, but a direct link to the farms and farmers of Coorg.

As we prepare to launch, we're acutely aware of the challenges ahead. Establishing a direct sourcing model is no small feat, especially for a new brand. But it's a challenge we embrace with entrepreneurial zeal. We're investing in relationships with farmers, developing transparent supply chain processes, and creating packaging that tells the story of each product's origin.

One of our most exciting initiatives is our partnership with local women's cooperative. As we work with them to bring their herbal infusions to market, we're also helping them develop business skills. "FarmtoCups is not just buying our products," Lakshmi Aunty says, "they're empowering us to become entrepreneurs in our own right."

As we stand on the brink of our launch, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey. When you choose FarmtoCups, you're not just selecting a beverage – you're becoming part of a movement that values transparency, sustainability, and exceptional taste.
