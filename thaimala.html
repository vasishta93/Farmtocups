THAIMALA ROAST

Thaimale Roast draws its name from the Kodava word for "Mother Hill," paying homage to Coorg’s sacred, life-giving hills. This 100% Robusta coffee captures the bold, full-bodied essence of Coorg, crafted with respect for the rich, nurturing spirit of the Western Ghats. Experience a powerful, pure cup that honors tradition with every sip.

Roast Level: Medium (Agtron scale: 55-60)
Grind Size: Medium (500-700 microns)
Ideal for: South Indian filter coffee, French press, and cold brew.

Size : 100 Gms (Sampler Pack)
Sampler pack can make 5-6 cups of coffee based on use. Includes 1 flavouring element.
